1: The battery life of electric cars doesn't allow for far travel



Electric cars would need to be "refilled" more frequently than non-electric cars because over time the battery degrades more and store less electricity.


2: Recharging can take several hours



Putting electricity into an electric cars does take a long time so that it can get full on electricty.


3: Electric cars are more expensive than combustion cars



The battery to make the car run is very expensive to get. It would also be expensive to replace the battery since the battery does degrade over time.
